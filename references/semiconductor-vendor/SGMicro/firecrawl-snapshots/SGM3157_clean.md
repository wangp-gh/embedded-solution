# SGM3157
Active
## 6Ω, Low Voltage SPDT Analog Switch in 6-Pin SC70
[![file](https://www.sg-micro.com/file.svg?fit=cover&width=64&format=webp&quality=75)SGM3157.pdf](https://www.sg-micro.com/rect/assets/a22623ad-a59a-4732-b010-4b667f6b9a7c/SGM3157.pdf?access_token=JB0xlRQ-10F_vQg1XXqsnx8zy4iF65LO)
Free Samples Request
![Preview Image](https://www.sg-micro.com/api/assets/939cc217-53d2-4976-b4ae-55ec986ce35a?access_token=JB0xlRQ-10F_vQg1XXqsnx8zy4iF65LO?fit=cover&width=3840&format=webp&quality=75)
The SGM3157 is a bidirectional, SPDT (single-pole/double-throw), CMOS [analog switch](https://www.sg-micro.com/products/analog-switches). It operates from a 1.8V to 5.5V single power supply.
The SGM3157 features low on-resistance, low voltage and high bandwidth. The high performances make it very suitable for multiple applications, such as portable equipment, audio and video signal routing, etc. Low power consumption is also one of the important reasons that make it a good choice.
The SGM3157 is available in a Green SC70-6 package. It operates over an ambient temperature range of -40℃ to +85℃.
More
FEATURES
- Single Supply Voltage Range: 1.8V to 5.5V
- Low On-Resistance: 6Ω (TYP) at V+ = 4.5V
- Fast Switching Times:
tON: 20ns (TYP)
tOFF: 15ns (TYP)
- Low On-Resistance Flatness
- -3dB Bandwidth: 300MHz
- High Off-Isolation: -51dB at 10MHz
- TTL/CMOS Compatible
- Rail-to-Rail Input and Output Operation
- Break-Before-Make Switching
- -40℃ to +85℃ Operating Temperature Range
- Available in a Green SC70-6 Package

Portable Equipment
Computer Peripherals
Sample-and-Hold Circuits
Personal Digital Assistants
