# i.MX RT Crossover MCUs
The i.MX RT crossover MCUs combine ease of use with high-performance processing. The i.MX RT series
offers many variants that propel industrial, IoT and automotive applications while delivering high
levels of integration and security, with optimal power consumption. i.MX RT Crossover MCUs are supported
by the [MCUXpresso ecosystem](https://www.nxp.com/design/design-center/software/development-software/mcuxpresso-software-and-tools-:MCUXPRESSO), which includes an SDK, a
choice of IDEs and secure provisioning and configuration tools to enable rapid development. Zephyr RTOS
enablement is also available.
