healthcare devices, wearable sports and fitness devices, AV remote controls, computer keyboards and mice, gaming

The KW31Z Wireless MCU integrates a 2.4 GHz transceiver supporting FSK/GFSK modulations, an Arm®
Cortex®-M0+ CPU, up to 512 KB Flash and up to 128 KB SRAM, Bluetooth Low Energy Link Layer hardware,
hardware security and peripherals optimized to meet the requirements of the target applications.

### Features

### Bluetooth Low Energy Radio Features

- 2.4 GHz Bluetooth Low Energy version 4.2 Compliant
- Typical Receiver Sensitivity (Bluetooth Low Energy) = -95 dBm
- Programmable Transmitter Output Power up to +3.5 dBm

### Core and Memories

- Up to 48 MHz Arm® Cortex®-M0+ core
- Up to 512 KB Flash memory
- Up to 128 KB SRAM
- Excellent coexistence performance
- Integrated balun to reduce system cost and PCB board area

### Low Power Consumption and Operating Voltage Ranges

- Nine low-power modes to provide power optimization based on application requirements
- Typical Rx/Tx Current (DC/DC Enabled): 6.5 mA / 8.4 mA
- Bypass Voltage: 1.71V to 3.6V
- DCDC Converter Buck Configuration: 2.1V to 4.2V
- DCDC Converter Boost Configuration: 0.9V to 1.795V

### Analog modules

- 16-bit Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC)
- 12-bit Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC)
- 6-bit High Speed Analog Comparator (CMP)

### Security

- AES-128 Accelerator (AESA), True Random Number Generator (TRNG)

### Supported Software

- Bluetooth Low Energy Host Stack and Profiles
- Generic FSK Link Layer Software
- Kinetis® Software Development Kit (SDK)
- FreeRTOS kernel and a bare-metal non-preemptive task scheduler
- Support for MCUXpresso and IAR
