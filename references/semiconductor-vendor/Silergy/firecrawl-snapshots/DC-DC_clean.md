DC-DC
Silergy's DC-DC solution provides a compact and reliable power supply solution with high energy efficiency, high power density and modular design.
Silergy's DC-DC solution is widely used in fields from consumer electronics to industrial equipment. It helps all kinds of electronic products to reduce energy consumption and size with the advantages of high efficiency, energy saving, stability, reliability and small size.
