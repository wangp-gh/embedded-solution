## **Your next 8-bit MCU is a 32-bit. It’s called STM32C0. And the series is expanding.**

The STM32C0, ST's most affordable 32-bit microcontroller, breaks the cost barrier to the 32-it world and makes 32-bit capabilities accessible to all developers.

Powered by the industry-standard [Arm® Cortex®-M0+](https://www.st.com/content/st_com/en/arm-32-bit-microcontrollers/arm-cortex-m0-plus.html) core running at 48 MHz, the STM32C0 series offers microcontrollers providing from 16 to 256 Kbytes of flash memory, up to 36 Kbytes of RAM and packages ranging from 8 to 64 pins.

Setting the lowest STM32 price point ever, the STM32C0 series makes no compromise on design quality and delivers a performance far beyond the existing 8-bit architectures.

## **Highlighted features**

Compact, affordable, and reliable, the series features:

- A fast 12-bit ADC with hardware resolution up to 16-bit

- USB host or device crystal-less for STM32C071

- FDCAN function support for STM32C092

- Flexible mapping on the DMA channels

- Timers with advanced control capability

- Several communication peripherals, always including 2 UARTs, even on the smallest microcontrollers


The STM32C0 supports a temperature range up to 125°C and comes in 8- to 64-pin packages, such as WLCSP, SON, TSSOP, LQFP, and UFQFPN packages.
